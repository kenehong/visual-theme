Our Story

Wang Kimbap started as a tiny corner stall in 2003 with one belief: everyday food deserves royal presentation. Our founder grew up watching her grandmother roll kimbap with the same precision a master carpenter joins wood beams without a single nail.

We believe the same care that painted palace ceilings belongs in a plate of tteokbokki.

Every dish is made fresh to order. The rice is seasoned by hand. The gochujang sauce follows a recipe three generations old. The only thing fast about us is the service.
